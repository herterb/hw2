Admiral Nedward von Dumpling

Supreme Chief of the High Seas


Summary

Nedward, also known as "Fast Neddie", is an "entrepreneur" that sails the open seas in search of new fortunes. He has a reputation for plundering and being a scallywag. He is not a good team player - led a mutiny against his former captain. Currently courting the daughter of the Count of Martinique. Holds Letters of Marque from the Dutch and French governements. Despises the Spanish. British government has a bounty on his head for 10,000 gold doubloons Has contracted and subsequently beaten scurvy.
